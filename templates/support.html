Frequently Asked Questions

What is synthetic data generation?

Synthetic data generation is the process of creating artificial data that maintains the statistical properties and patterns of real data while ensuring privacy and confidentiality.

Which models are available?

We currently support three main models: Gaussian Copula, CTGAN (Conditional Tabular GAN), and TVAE (Tabular Variational Autoencoder).

How secure is my data?

Your data is encrypted during transmission and storage. We never share your original data, and all processing is done securely on our servers.

What file formats are supported?

Currently, we support CSV files. Make sure your data is properly formatted with headers and consistent data types.

How many samples can I generate?

You can generate up to 10,000 samples per project. For larger datasets, please contact our support team.

How accurate is the synthetic data?

Our models maintain statistical relationships and patterns from the original data while ensuring privacy. The accuracy depends on the chosen model and data characteristics.
